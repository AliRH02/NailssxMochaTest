Policies / Políticas

Appointments

Please arrive on time. A 20-minute grace period is allowed.

If you are running late, your session may be shortened or rescheduled.

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please give at least 48 hours notice.

No guests allowed.

Submit your inspiration photos the same day you book your appointment.

Deposits

A $10 deposit is required to secure your appointment.

Deposits are non-refundable. No deposit means no appointment.

Payments are due immediately after your appointment.

We accept Zelle and cash.
